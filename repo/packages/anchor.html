This is a beta! And also requires you to always be connected to the internet.

Please note. There are some problems with eclipse currently pertaining to long device resprings etc. I can insure Anchor is not the cause. Please help in the improvement of anchor and send in apropriate crash reports via the crash reporter app.

Current issues:
1)Settings panel incomplete.
2)Holding on an icon but not dragging and holding it to a spot messes up your layout.
3)Dragging icons out of a page will result in an empty page until you respring.
4)Deleting or updating apps may cause layouts to change.

Anchor is a tweak that allows you to place your icons anywhere on your home screen. You no longer need to worry about icons jumping back to the next available spot. Know you can instead place them as you please on your homescreen grid.
